OCLL Algs

tip: if you repeat the algs correctly, they'll cycle through other cases until they solve themselves

Name Case Number Alg Notation
Sune 1 (R U R') U (R U2 R') (1) takes out the pair, (2) reinserts it
Antisune 2 (R U2 R') U' (R U' R') (1) takes out the pair, (2) reinserts it
H 3 (R U R') U [R U' R' U] (R U2 R') (1) takes out the pair, [] we'll come back to later, (2) reinserts it
L 4 (R U R') U {R U' R' U}*2 (R U2 R') (1) takes out the pair, repeat {} twice, (2) reinserts it
T 5 [R U2 R' U'R U'R'] U [R U R' U R U2 R'] [Antisune] adjust [Sune]
U 6 [R U R' U R U2] R2 (U' R U' R' U2 R) [Sune] R2 into (Backsune)
Pi 7 R U2 R2 U' R2 U' R2 U2 R This alg removes 2 pairs at a time. Good luck!
